Frequently Asked Questions

What if my phone battery dies?

You can contact the hostel staff for assistance. They can help you access your pass information.

How do I apply for a pass?

Download the SVCE Hostels app from the links provided on the website. Once installed, open the app and follow the instructions to apply for a pass.

How long is my QR code valid?

Your QR code is valid from half an hour before your pass start time until half an hour after your pass end time.

Can I apply for a pass in advance?

Yes, you can apply for a pass in advance. We recommend applying at least a few hours before you need the pass to ensure timely approval.

Who do I contact if I have trouble with the app?

You can contact the Hostel App Team for support. Their contact information is available on the website's footer.

What if I need to change my pass details after it's been approved?

Contact your Resident Tutor immediately. They may be able to modify the pass details or cancel the existing pass so you can apply for a new one.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is secure. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information.

Do I need an internet connection to use the app?

You will need an internet connection to apply for a pass and receive approval. However, once you have your QR code, you do not need an internet connection to scan it at the gate.
